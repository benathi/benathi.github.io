# Ben Athiwaratkun

## Bio

I am a Senior Director at Together AI, where I lead the Core ML (Turbo) Research and Development team focusing on full-stack inference efficiency — from low-level optimization such as sparsity, quantization, efficient architecture, and speculative decoding, to high-level optimization such as smart routing (ThunderAgent), multi-model optimization (Mixture of Agents and Squeeze Evolve).

Previously, I was an ML Scientist at AWS AI Labs, working on few-shot learning, language models for code generation, and efficient inference. I completed my PhD in Statistics (with a focus on machine learning) from Cornell University, under the supervision of Andrew Gordon Wilson. Before my PhD, I studied Mathematics, Physics, and Economics at Williams College.
